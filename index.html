Hi there! Thank you for checking out my website! My name is Austin and I'm a 16-year-old with a passion for aviation. I enjoy flying all around the world and experiencing tons of different cultures. However, in my future I hope to become an Air Traffic Controller after I finish college.

The Heist



The Heist is a project of mine that is still in development. It is a game for the Roblox platform involving stealing items without being detected. It is planned to have story mode and multiple other challenging gamemodes!

Ground Crew Simulator



Ground Crew Simulator is another project of mine also in development for the Roblox Platform. This game is meant to be a game similar to Cabin Crew Simulator, but obviously for ground crew. We want to try and make this as realistic as we can, while also allowing for good performance on all devices.
